Objective

At the end of the first semester, you are expected to have a proper grasp of the basic concepts that will shape up a foundation for your accelerated learning through the next 2 semesters. You will have a good understanding of the basics of computing, computers, data structures, and algorithms. In addition to this, you will be well grounded in the fundamentals of programming, basic HTML, CSS and JavaScript. The topics are organised into monthly modules with weekly components.
